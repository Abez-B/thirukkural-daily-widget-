A cross-platform daily Thirukkural flashcard widget. Cycles through all 1330 Kurals, showing one perfectly-formatted Kural each day with native Tamil typography and expert explanations.

Features:
• Fully offline functionality: No internet connection required.
• Open Source: 100% free and open-source software with no trackers or ads.
• Widget Customization: Extensive customization options including beautiful monochromatic themes, typography scaling, and multiple expert commentaries.
• Multiple Sizes: Fully resizable widgets that gracefully adapt to different home screen layouts.
• Battery-friendly: Native zero-battery epoch cycle math with a completely invisible background-only footprint.

<b>Note: The content of this app (Kurals and explanations) is available in Tamil and English.</b>
