A cross-platform daily Thirukkural flashcard widget. Cycles through all 1330 Kurals, showing one perfectly-formatted Kural each day with native Tamil typography and Dr. Kalaignar's explanations. Features a completely invisible background-only footprint and native zero-battery epoch cycle math.

<b>Note: The content of this app (Kurals and explanations) is currently available exclusively in Tamil.</b>
